## Short Summary

Swiggy, a food delivery platform in India, implemented a two-stage fine-tuning approach for language models to improve search relevance in their hyperlocal food delivery service. They first performed unsupervised fine-tuning using historical search queries and order data, followed by supervised fine-tuning with manually curated query-item pairs. The solution uses the all-mpnet-base-v2 model and achieved superior MAP and Precision@5 metrics compared to baseline models, while meeting strict latency requirements of 100ms for real-time query processing.
